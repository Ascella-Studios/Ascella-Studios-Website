Privacy Policy

BookStreak — developed by Ascella Studios

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Overview

BookStreak is a reading habit tracker that stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect personal information, create user accounts, or store your reading data on any server.

Data Stored on Your Device

BookStreak stores the following data locally using on-device storage (SQLite). This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it:

  • Books you add (title, author, page count, cover image URL, notes)
  • Reading sessions (pages read, dates)
  • App settings (daily goal, theme preference, notification preferences)
  • Onboarding completion status

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • We do not require account creation or login
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other device data
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties for advertising purposes

Third-Party Services

BookStreak uses the following third-party services:

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat processes your purchase through Apple's App Store and may collect anonymized purchase data (subscription status, transaction identifiers) to manage your subscription. RevenueCat does not have access to your reading data. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Expo (Diagnostics)

The app uses Expo's development framework, which may collect anonymized crash reports and diagnostic data to help us improve app stability. This data does not include your reading history or personal information.

Open Library API

When you add a book by ISBN, BookStreak queries the Open Library API to retrieve book metadata (title, author, cover image URL). This request includes the ISBN you entered but no personal information.

AI Reading Insights

BookStreak Pro includes AI-powered reading insights. When this feature is used, anonymized reading statistics (pages read, streak length, daily goal — no book titles, notes, or personal information) are sent to our AI proxy server to generate personalized reading tips. This data is not stored on our servers and is discarded after generating the response.

Data Export

BookStreak Pro allows you to export your reading data as a CSV file via your device's share sheet. This export is initiated by you and the data goes only where you choose to send it.

Data Deletion

All BookStreak data is stored locally on your device. To delete all data, simply uninstall the app. Uninstalling removes all books, reading sessions, settings, and preferences permanently.

Children's Privacy

BookStreak does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
