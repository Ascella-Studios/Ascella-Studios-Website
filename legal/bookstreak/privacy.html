Privacy Policy

BookStreak
Effective Date: March 8, 2026
Last Updated: March 8, 2026

Overview

BookStreak is built by Ascella Studios. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data BookStreak collects, how it's used, and your rights.

Data Collection

BookStreak stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Data Stored on Your Device

  • Books you add (title, author, page count, reading status)
  • Reading sessions (date, pages read)
  • App settings (daily page goal, preferences)
  • Streak and progress data

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email, or any account information
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Browsing history
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes

Third-Party Services

BookStreak uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — Manages subscription purchases. RevenueCat processes payment data through Apple's App Store. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Open Library API — Used to search for books when adding to your library. Search queries (book titles/authors) are sent to openlibrary.org. No personal data is transmitted.
  • Expo — May collect anonymous crash diagnostics to help us fix bugs. No personal data is included.

AI Reading Coach (Pro Feature)

If you use the AI Reading Coach, your reading data (page counts, book titles, session history) is sent to our AI service to generate personalized insights. This data is not stored on our servers and is used only to generate your response. No personal identifying information is sent.

Data Deletion

All your data is stored locally on your device. To delete all data:

  • Use the "Reset All Data" option in Settings, or
  • Uninstall the app — this permanently removes all stored data

Children's Privacy

BookStreak does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to use.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
